Homeschool Helper Angular - .Net Core

Homeschool Helper is an app that I created to record school hours for those who homeschool. The backend was created with C# and ASP.NET Core. The frontend was made with Angular 9 and Angular Material.

Features
  • Export recorded hours to PDF
  • Record homeschool hours by customizable subjects.
  • Data chart to visualize the weekly hours for each student.
